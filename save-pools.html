Save The Pools

Graphic Design

This project was done for my Graphic Studio class in second year. The goal of this project was to create a bus shelter ad to bring awareness to an issue within Thunder Bay at the time. I decided to show to story of a girl protesting to save Dease Pool, a popular outdoor pool that was on the verge of being shut down by the city.

For this ad I wanted to display a story to envoke more emotion. I used multiple illustrations and soft colours to show the girl's life and how she has been affected.
